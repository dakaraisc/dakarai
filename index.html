I'm a second-year ECE Ph.D. student and a graduate research assistant in the RoboTouch Lab at the CS department at UIUC. I received my bachelor's and master's degrees in Mechanical Engineering from the University of Tehran. My research primarily focuses on robotic tactile sensing, perception, manipulation, and sensor design.
